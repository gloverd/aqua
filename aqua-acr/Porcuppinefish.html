  • Porcupinefish

    Porcuppinefish

    Small dark spots over entire body and fins.

    Long spintes are erect only when inflated

    Balloonfish

    Porcuppinefish

    Long spintes on head, Small dark spots on body, but NO spots on fins.

    Iris is yellow, pupil is iridescent blue-green specks. Spines usually lowered but become erect when inflated.

    Striped Burrfish

    Porcuppinefish

    Irregular pattern of dark brown, more-or less parallel stripes on back and sides.

    Iris is yellow-gold and pupil has iridescent blue-green specks. Spines always erect.
