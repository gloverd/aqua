  • Atlantic Creolefish

    Bass/Seabass

    Bring red spot at base of pectoral fin.

    Three light or dark spots along back

    Tobaccofish

    Bass/Seabass

    Midbody is shades of orange to brown (tobacco color). Dark "U" shaped border on tail.

    Alternating sseries of dark and white to yellow or salmon blotches along back. White belly.

    Harlequin Bass

    Bass/Seabass

    Dark tiger-like body bars. Upper body has whiteish undercolor, lower body yellow

    Head more pointed that other members of the seabass family.

    Fairy Basslet

    Basslet

    Bicolored -- purple to violet front and yellow to gold rear. Dark spot on dorsal fin.

    Several streaks on head run across eye

    Glassy Cooper Sweeper

    Bigeyes

    Shiny copper color. Large eyes.

    Deep belly profile and very thin body. Dark band at base of anal fin

    Scrawled Filefish

    Filefish

    Covered with blue to blue-green spots. Irregular lines and black dots.

    Elongated body with long broom-like tail

    Orangespotted Filefish

    Filefish

    In most common phase they have whide, dark brown stripes and narrow dull yellow strips that converge near tail base.

    White spot on upper base of tail and usually a second smaller spent underneath. Orangish spots cover body.

    Neon Goby

    Goby

    Electric blue body stripe runs from front of eye eye to base of tail.

    No markings on snout

    Lookdown

    Jack

    Very thin body and extremely blunt head. May display three or four body bars.

    Fore part of dorsal and anal fins elongated.

    Bar Jack

    Jack

    Silvery Body.

    Bright blue and black border on tail runs along dorsal fin and onto lower tail fin

    Saucereye Porgy

    Porgy

    Silvery with blueish yellowish and or brassy cast, often with yellow wash over head and forebody.

    Short bluish saucer shaped line below eye, and blotch at upper base of pectoral fin. Corners of mouth are yellow.

    Mahogany Snapper

    Snapper

    Silver towhite, often with reddish tinge. Reddish margin on tail (may quite narrow and pale).

    Redish border on dorsal and anal fins. Often have a dark spot below rear dorsal fin.

    Atlantic Spadefish

    Spadefish

    Silvery body with several dark bars.

    Body shape resembles a "spade" on playing cards

    Glasseye Snapper

    Sweeper

    Always have silver bars on back which may be faint

    Body varies from red to pale, silvery, moddled pink.

    Sand tilefish

    Tilefish

    Long white body.

    Crescent tail. Dark area on upper central tail.

    Queen Triggerfish

    Triggerfish

    Body color in various shades of purple, blue turquoise and green; lower half of head yellow or yellowish.

    Streaming tips on rear dorsal and tail fins. Two blue stripes on face. Small lines radiate from around eye.

    Sargassum Triggerfish

    Triggerfish

    Body varies from blueish grey to brownish-grey

    Three dark lines on cheek. Series of sashes form thin stripes on body. White dot just forward of eye with white crescent bordering upper eye

    Trumpetfish

    Trumpetfish

    Long thin body.

    Trumpet-like mouth
