Doctorfish

Surgeonfish

Body can change from blueish gray to dark brown, and pale or darken dramatically. Always have 10-12 body bars although they may be faint.

Dark area extends from base of pectoral fin outward along leading edge.

Ocean Surgeonfish

Surgeonfish

Body of uniform color with no body bars. Pectoral fin clearish often with yellow tints.

Markings radiate from eye. Blue or white edging on dorsal, anal, and tail fins

Surgeonfish

Body blue overall; juvenile bright yellow.

White or yellow spine on base of tail. More circular than other surgeonfish.
