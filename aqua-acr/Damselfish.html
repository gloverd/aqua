    Beaugregory

    Damselfish

    Dusky grey to blueish body with areas of yellow, especially on tail, rear dorsal and anal fins.

    Series of blue dots on head and gill cover continue as vague horizontal lines on upper body. Rarely have black spot on tail base.

    Beaugregory - Juvenile

    Damselfish

    Black spot ringed in blue on dorsal fin.

    Blue wash extending from upper head over dorsal fin and back

    Cocoa Damselfish

    Damselfish

    Usually have a dark spot on tail base

    Lack of spot causes confusion with Beaugregory

    Cocoa Damselfish - Juvenile

    Damselfish

    Black spot ringed in blue on dorsal fin, extend slightly onto back

    Blue wash extending from upper head over dorsal fin and back

    Dusky Damselfish

    Damselfish

    Dorsal and anal fins rounded and rarely extend beyond base of tail.

    Narrow dark edge on anal fin.

    Dusky Damselfish - Juvenile

    Damselfish

    Brilliant orage wash extends from snout across nape & about halfway down dorsal fin.

    Large dark spot on dorsal fin & small dark spot on tail base.

    Threespot Damselfish

    Damselfish

    Body varies from yellow to tan & grey, darkening with age.

    Dark spot on base of pectoral fin. Lack saddle on upper base of tail.

    Threespot Damselfish - Juvenile

    Damselfish

    has large second spot on back and base of dorsal fin

    (disapears on maturity)

    Purple Reeffish

    Damselfish

    Slate blue to dark blue with bright blue markings on head and back.

    (Juveniles have brilliant blue with "V" on snout)

    Blue Chromis

    Damselfish

    Brilliant blue, often dark on nape and along back

    slender deeply forked tail with dark borders

    Brown Chromis

    Damselfish

    Brown body, border of dorsal fin and tips of tail fin yellow.Dark spots around base of pectoral fin

    Juveniles tail has black borders and white spots on base of dorsal fin

    Yellowtip Damselfish

    Damselfish

    Black with bright yellow rear body and tail; size of yellow area varies from upper half of tailfin to covering rear half of body.

    Pectoral fins translucent with yellow rays. Ventral and anal fins ofted edged in bright blue.

    Sergeant Major

    Damselfish

    Five black body bars. Upper lip does not overhand lower. Upper body usually Yellow with shades of green and blue.

    Male becomes dark blue to purple durign mating season
