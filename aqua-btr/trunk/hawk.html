  • Flame Hawkfish

    up to 3 ½ inches (9cm)

    Brilliant red in color, Broad zone of black on back

    Black ring around eye

    Freckled Hawkfish

    up to 8 ¾ inches (22cm)

    Usually brown in color, but colors can be variable

    Numerous freckles on head and forebody, Brown striped area towards rear of body

    Diagonal-Banded Sweetlips

    up to 19 inches (48cm)

    White with diagonal black body bands

    White belly, Yellow fins and lips, Spotted dorsal, anal, & tail fins

    Oriental Sweetlips

    up to 3 feet (85cm)

    White with black stripes, Yellow fins and lips, Spotted dorsal, anal, and tail fins,

    Juvenile: dark brown with several large irregular white spots

    Orbicular Batfish

    up to 50 cm

    Thin and disc shaped, Silvery gray

    Black bars through pectoral fin & eye; Black margin on anal and dorsal fins
